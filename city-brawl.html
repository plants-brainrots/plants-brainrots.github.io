About City Brawl

City Brawl is an exhilarating casual action fighting game that immerses players in the gritty streets of a bustling city. As a street fighter, you’ll battle against waves of gang members using martial arts moves, aiming to knock down as many enemies as possible while avoiding their attacks. With each victory, you earn fruits that help level up your character’s skills, making you a more powerful fighter.

City Brawl Gameplay Walkthrough

Set against a vibrant urban backdrop, City Brawl drops you into intense combat scenarios where you face off against relentless gangs. Each level presents new challenges, with tougher enemies and formidable bosses. Utilize your character’s unique fighting combos and special attacks to defeat your foes and collect fruits to enhance your abilities.

How To Play City Brawl

Select Your Character And Difficulty Level

Begin your journey by choosing one of the 9 available characters, each boasting distinct punch and kick powers. Depending on your experience level, select a difficulty setting—“easy” or “normal” for newcomers, or “hard” and “insane” for seasoned players seeking a challenge.

Engage In Battle

Once you’re ready, jump straight into the action. Enemies will rush towards you, and it’s your job to fend them off. Use a combination of punches, kicks, and special moves to take down the gangs. Be mindful of each enemy’s strength and attack style, and don’t forget to shield yourself from incoming hits. The game ends if your health bar depletes, so stay alert!

Collect Oranges And Unlock Characters

Oranges serve as the in-game currency, primarily earned by defeating enemies. Collect these to upgrade your fighter’s health, weapon techniques, and attack power. Additionally, use oranges to unlock new heroes, each with unique stats and combat styles, enhancing your gameplay experience.

Progress Through Levels

Complete a level by defeating all gang members, unlocking tougher challenges as you advance. It’s crucial to power up your character between rounds to ensure you’re prepared for the escalating difficulty of upcoming battles.

Conclusion: Join the Fight In City Brawl!

Are you ready to unleash your inner street fighter? Dive into City Brawl today and experience the thrill of battling against gangs in an action-packed environment.
