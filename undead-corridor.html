About Undead Corridor

Are you ready to face relentless waves of zombies in a thrilling survival shooter? Undead Corridor is a fast-paced 2D game where players must navigate confined spaces, such as corridors and rooms, to eliminate hordes of undead. Your survival depends on upgrading weapons, managing ammo, and maintaining precise aim while avoiding being overwhelmed by increasing numbers of zombies.

Fierce Combat Against the Undead

In Undead Corridor, you step into the shoes of a lone survivor during a zombie apocalypse. Your mission is to clear buildings infested with zombies and stay alive for as long as possible. The game features multiple modes set in various intense environments, including offices, hospitals, and streets, each presenting unique survival challenges. As you defeat waves of zombies, you earn in-game currency to purchase powerful weapons, enhancing your chances of survival.

Undead Corridor Key Features

Undead Corridor offers an adrenaline-pumping experience for players who love zombie survival games. With its engaging gameplay and intense environments, players can expect a variety of challenges that keep the action fresh and exciting.

Some standout features that enhance the gameplay:
  • Choose from various modes, each presenting unique survival challenges in different settings.
  • Encounter a range of zombies, including faster and tougher variants, that require different strategies to defeat.
  • Unlock and upgrade a wide selection of weapons, allowing for customized combat styles and improved effectiveness.
  • Experience thrilling gore effects and unique ragdoll physics that add to the immersive atmosphere of the game.

How To Play Undead Corridor

  • Start your mission by choosing a game mode that suits your skill level.
  • Click or tap to aim and shoot at approaching zombies. Move strategically to maintain visibility and eliminate threats efficiently.
  • Keep an eye on your ammunition and reload wisely. Switch between weapons as needed to adapt to the situation.
  • The closer zombies get, the more dangerous they become. Be prepared to shoot rapidly or kick them back if they get too close.
  • As you clear corridors and survive longer, earn in-game currency to invest in better weapons for tougher challenges in later levels.

Game Controls

Tap or Click: Shoot your weapon.
Spacebar: Kick the zombies back.
R: Reload your weapon.
Q: Switch between weapons.
